THE VISION

The company's goal is to create and provide customers with a set of convenient and highly effective tools for solving various problems.

DTV offers a comprehensive solution for security management, recording, and storage of property rights for both movable and immovable real assets. Our multifunction platform provides government-verified digital documents and enables public and private entities to generate and manage secure registries of digital IDs (NFTs). By utilizing distributed storage and security features, DTV minimizes power and network congestion commonly associated with blockchain technology.
