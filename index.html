Welcome to Our Practice

When you are choosing a dentist, there are a few things to consider. You need to ensure their prices are affordable, the dentist is experienced and reputable, and the facility is state-of-the-art. At Affordable Dental Care, Dr. Ilya Yagudayev and our entire team put patients first, offering comprehensive treatments, advanced technology, and convenient financing options in a relaxing, welcoming environment. In Riverhead, NY, we provide everything your smile needs to thrive at its optimal health.

Our goal is to provide affordable dentistry for each patient, no matter their financial status or background. We believe that all patients should experience their own optimal oral health.

Finance Your Dream Smile
