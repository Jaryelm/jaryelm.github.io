Shaping The Future

Time: 5pm to 11pm

Sponsored by Delnorte (DTV), we are excited to invite you to our upcoming blockchain event, where we will delve into the crucial topic of migrating real estate assets to non-fungible tokens NFTs. This event will highlight the significance of raising awareness about this innovative approach, which enables real-world assets to be securely recorded on the blockchain. By leveraging NFTs, we can enhance transparency and fortify protection against fraudulent activities in the real estate industry.

​We are bringing prominent leaders together to discuss the most pressing issues on how they are dealing with new methods of ownership through tokenization, legislation and investment opportunities.

​Selected Featured Speakers from Saudi Arabia and USA main players in Tech investments.
